# 4. Capabilities: Batch Updates for Earnings & Deductions

1. Click Batch Update. Under type, earnings are flashed as default.

<div align="center">

![alt text](images/image-14.png)

</div>

2. Click the Type Drop-down Box, and select Earnings.
3. Select what type of earnings to update.

<div align="center">

![alt text](images/image-15.png)

</div>

4. Manually enter the amount earned by the employee.
   The user can also search the name of the employee and enter the amount earned by the employee.

5. Click the Apply button.

<div align="center">

![alt text](images/image-16.png)

</div>

6. Click the Type Drop-down Box, and Select Deductions.
7. Select what type of deductions to update.

<div align="center">

![alt text](images/image-17.png)

</div>

8. Click SSS.
9. Click Re-Calc All Button.
10. Click the Apply button.

<div align="center">

![alt text](images/image-18.png)

</div>
