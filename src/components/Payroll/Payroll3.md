# 3. Capabilities: Adding Employees from the Created Payroll Transaction and Uploading of Biometrics

1. Click the Add Employee button.

<div align="center">

![alt text](images/image-10.png)

</div>

2. Manually select or check the employees for the transaction.

<div align="center">

![alt text](images/image-11.png)

</div>

3. Click Add Selected.
4. Click Import Time Logs.
5. Drag or click the time logs document to upload.
6. To see highlighted cells in the imported time logs, click legends.
7. Click the Save & Apply button.

<div align="center">

![alt text](images/image-12.png)

</div>

8. In case of wrong file upload, click the Time Logs button, then Another Import.
9. Drag or click the time logs document to upload for update.
10. Click the Override button to override the time logs
11. Click the Skip button to retain the current logs.
12. Click the Save & Apply button.

![alt text](images/image-13.png)

</div>
